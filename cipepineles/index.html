About

Cipe Pineles was born in Vienna, Austria in 1908 and immigrated with her mother and sisters to New York in 1915. Eventually she would go to high school in brooklyn and attend Pratt University . At Pratt University she was awarded the Louis Comfort Tiffany Foundation Scholarship. And, after college Cipe spent an “adventurous” year in the still-life painting business.

Magazine Buisness

In 1947 Cipe moved to Seventeen magazine as their art director. In the beginning she commissioned fiction illustrations on the theory that young kids would have few barriers accepting fine art. As she continued her work she began working with her editor Helen Valentine. Together they created magazines for teenage girls to give them something to be a part of. Helen was able to advertise and create success for this magazine and Cipe created elegant and lively covers which I will show more of now.
